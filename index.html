Numerant — Privacy Policy Last updated: June 2026 Numerant ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Numerant app. 1. Information We Collect We collect information you provide directly: financial transactions, investment data, business records, and account credentials (email or Google account). We do not collect any information without your explicit input. 2. How We Use Your Information Your data is used solely to provide Numerant's features — tracking finances, investments, and business records. AI features (bank statement parsing, investment analysis, financial health scoring) send relevant data to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. This data is not stored by Anthropic beyond the session. 3. Data Storage All data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, hosted in Europe (europe-west2 region). Data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard TLS protocols. 4. Third-Party Services Firebase (Google) — authentication and database storage Anthropic Claude API — AI analysis features RevenueCat — subscription and payment management Yahoo Finance, CoinGecko — live investment price data Open Exchange Rates — live currency conversion rates 5. Data Sharing We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Data is only shared with the services listed above as strictly necessary to operate the app. 6. Data Deletion You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through Settings in the app, or by contacting us at support@numerant.app. Deletion is permanent and irreversible. 7. Security We use Firebase's enterprise-grade security, optional PIN lock, and encrypted cloud sync to protect your data. We recommend enabling the PIN lock feature for additional protection of your financial information. 8. Children's Privacy Numerant is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. 9. Changes to This Policy We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. Continued use of Numerant after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. 10. Contact Us For privacy questions or data requests, please contact us at: support@numerant.app Numerant — Track it. Own it. Grow it.
