Privacy Policy for Numerant Last updated: June 2026 1. Information We Collect Numerant collects information you provide directly: transactions, investments, business data, and account credentials. We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage. 2. How We Use Your Information Your financial data is used solely to provide the app's features. We use Anthropic's Claude API to analyse uploaded bank statements and investments. Data sent to Claude is not stored beyond the session. 3. Data Storage All data is stored in Firebase Firestore, hosted in Europe (europe-west2). Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. 4. Third-Party Services - Firebase (Google) — authentication and database - Anthropic Claude API — AI analysis features - RevenueCat — subscription management - Yahoo Finance, CoinGecko — live price data - Open Exchange Rates — currency conversion 5. Data Sharing We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except as required to operate the app (listed above). 6. Data Deletion You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at support@numerant.app or through the app settings. 7. Children's Privacy Numerant is not intended for users under 18 years of age. 8. Contact For privacy questions: support@numerant.app 9. Changes We may update this policy. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
